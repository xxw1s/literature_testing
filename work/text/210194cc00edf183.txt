2007 02 2007 INITIATORS & PYROTECHNICS 1003-1480 2007 01-0048-03 EFP 603 TJ410.33 X EFP EFP 1 603 603 A Tests on the Law of EFP Penetrating 603 Armor CAO Bing (Mechanical Engineering College, NUST, Nanjing, 210094) Abstract On the basis of the pulse X-ray photograph and recovering fragment behind the target, the damage phenomena of limited thickness 603 armor which is penetrated by EFP is studied. At the meantime, the law of penetrating EFP 603 armor with limited thickness is analyzed. After that, an empirical formula on the limited penetration velocity of EFP penetrating 603 armor is established.
